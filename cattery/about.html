About us

Welcome to Irish Snowflake Cattery, your premier destination for exceptional British Longhairs and Shorthairs. Established in 2023, we are dedicated to providing the highest standards of care and breeding for our cherished cats.

Our mission is to produce healthy, happy kittens that showcase the best traits of their breeds. We focus on champion bloodlines, ensuring each kitten inherits not only stunning physical qualities but also friendly and loving temperaments. Every cat in our cattery is carefully selected for its health and character, following ethical breeding practices to maintain the integrity of these wonderful breeds.

At Irish Snowflake Cattery, we believe every kitten deserves a loving home. We take pride in socializing our kittens, making sure they are well-adjusted and ready to become cherished members of your family. Our commitment extends beyond breeding; we aim to create lifelong connections between our kittens and their new families.

We invite you to explore our cattery and learn about our available kittens. Discover the joy of welcoming a Snowflake companion into your home!

If you have any questions or would like to know more about our breeding practices and available kittens, please don't hesitate to contact us.
